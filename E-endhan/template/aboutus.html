Even as India moves towards a cleaner and greener economy, the ubiquitous generators set, being an emergency power backup solution, is here to stay.

However, generators sets are taken for granted with little or practically no thought spared for issues like fuel management.

Fuel management systems are employed in almost every industry to monitor, control and maintain the fuel stock. Generators powered by diesel fuel are known for their fuel efficiency.

In the event of a power outage or when the power grid is disconnected, these generators provide a backup power supply system for homes, businesses, and offices.

Moreover, using the wrong fuel grade or failing to test the quality of the diesel can cause a great deal of malfunction within the genset.

Additionally, it is common for people to simply store fuel until they need it and use it as needed, which in turn leads to various problems. A good fuel management strategy is therefore necessary to ensure the smooth functioning of diesel gensets.
