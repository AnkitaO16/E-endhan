Tow Service

A Tow Services (also called a wrecker, a breakdown truck, recovery vehicle or a breakdown lorry) is a Service used to move disabled, improperly parked, impounded, or otherwise indisposed motor vehicles. This may involve recovering a vehicle damaged in an accident, returning one to a drivable surface in a mishap or inclement weather, or towing or transporting one via flatbed to a repair shop or other location.
